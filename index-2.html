Wooper

Because it looks like an axolotl and axolotls are the best.

  • Looks like an upright, armless axolotl.
  • Lives in cold water.
  • While walking on land, it coats its body with a slimy, toxic film that keeps its skin from dehydrating and insulates against the cold.

The many stories about the Woopers

A Wooper was close friends with Ash's Goodra when they lived together in the wetlands between Lumiose City and Laverre City. It made semi-regular appearances in the XY series, particularly in episodes revolving around Goodra.
